Study on the Impact of Alternative Fuels on Gasoline Consumption

As part of my previous major, I took an intro to Data Science class (CMSE 201) where the capstone project required us to come up with a question and answer it utilizing data. The question I picked was "Is Gasoline Mutually Exclusive with Alternative Fuel?" to which I found some pretty interesting results. All the code shown was produced by me unless explicitly stated otherwise.

3D Designed and Printed Phone Case

In my freshman year of College, I was in the College of Engineering which required me to take the EGR100 class, in which we were tasked to design and optionally print a phone case.

I worked with my team to complete this task, where I designed the final product and printed it utilizing TPU filament due to its high elasticity, which better protected the phone from damage.
